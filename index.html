ABOUT US

OUR AIM

So many are without hope and loose motivation therefore, We at Height of Hope aim to Inspire You to Inspire others. Only the love and sacrifice of God can bring true peace to the heart of men.

OUR MOTTO

We live by the word of God and not the opinion of men. 

OUR PLEDGE TO YOU

Height of Hope pledge to educate, train, and bring hope to the hopeless, be a comfort to the comfortless. Firstly, by reaching out to and bringing hope to those in despair. Secondly, by bringing the pure word of God as found in the bible.
